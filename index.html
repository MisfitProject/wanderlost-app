FAQ

Frequently
Asked.

Everything you need to know.

What is Wanderlost?

Wanderlost is an AI-powered local discovery app. Tap the compass orb and we'll find a hidden gem near you — a café, restaurant, park, bar, gallery, or any interesting place that locals love.

How does discovery work?

We use your current GPS location and the Google Maps Places API to find highly-rated places within 5km. Our algorithm prioritizes hidden gems over well-known chains, filtering by rating, category, and novelty.

Is it free?

Yes! Free users get 3 discoveries per session. Premium unlocks unlimited discoveries, saved places, and full history for 20 CHF/month or 200 CHF/year.

Why does it need my location?

Location is essential to find places near you. We only access your location when you actively tap Discover — never in the background. Your coordinates are never stored on our servers.

Can I use it abroad?

Absolutely! Wanderlost works anywhere Google Maps has coverage. It's designed for travelers and locals alike — discover the best of any city in the world.

How do I cancel Premium?

Go to Settings → Membership → Manage. You can cancel anytime and keep access until the end of your billing period. Refunds available within 14 days.
